how to play

  1. The board is 5×5. Only the lit 3×3 grid is in play — the grid moves.
  2. Each player has 4 pieces. Your first two turns must be placements, on empty lit cells.
  3. Once both players have placed two pieces, a turn is one of:
    • Place a remaining piece on an empty lit cell.
    • Slide the grid one step in any direction (it stays on the board).
    • Move one of your pieces — from anywhere, even the dark — to an empty lit cell.
  4. Make three in a row of your pieces inside the lit grid to win. Pieces in the dark don't count.
  5. If a grid slide lights up three-in-a-row for both players at once, the game is a tie.
